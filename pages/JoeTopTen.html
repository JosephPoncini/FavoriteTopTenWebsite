The Top 10:

Joseph Poncini Picks

Number 10

Sheep

Shop Tier: 3, Stats: 2/2

The sheep takes number one my list because of its ability to summon more sheep works very well when combined with pets who give bonuses when friends are summoned. Teams can only have a max of 5 pets though at a time, so be careful that your sheep have room to be summoned when going into battle

Level 1 Faint: Summon two 2/2 Ram.
Level 2 Faint: Summon two 4/4 Ram.
Level 3 Faint: Summon two 6/6 Ram.

Shop Tier: 3, Stats: 1/3

The Giraffe takes 9 on my list because of its ability to add health to its teammates each round. Being a Tier 3 pet, this allows friendly pets to start getting buff early on in the game

Level 1: End turn: Give the nearest friend ahead +1 Attack and +1 Health.
Level 2: End turn: Give the nearest 2 friends ahead +1 Attack and +1 Health.
Level 3: End turn: Give the nearest 3 friends ahead +1 Attack and +1 Health.

Number 9

Giraffe

Number 8

Deer

SShop Tier: 4, Stats: 1/1

The deer is a personal favorite of mine. It takes its place on the list because of its ability to summon a bus when fainting. The bus with the chili perk is able to attack two enemy pets at once and can do serious damage to an early and mid game team. The deer probably won't win you any late game trophies but can win you 3 to 4 mid game ones

Level 1 Faint: Summon one 5/5 Bus with chili.
Level 2 Faint: Summon one 10/10 Bus with chili
Level 3 Faint: Summon one 15/15 Bus with chili

Shop Tier: 3, Stats: 2/4

The camel takes 7 on the list. It is a Tier 3 that can be very strong and integral even in the late game. As the camel gets hurt, it buffs the friendly pet behind it. If you can get a tanky camel in the front of your team, your second pet can become a complete powerhouse!

Level 1: Hurt: Give the nearest friend behind +2 Attack and +2 Health.
Level 2: Hurt: Give the nearest friend behind +4 Attack and +4 Health.
Level 3: Hurt: Give the nearest friend behind +6 Attack and +6 Health.

Number 7

Camel

Number 6

Peacock

Shop Tier: 2, Stats: 2/5

The peacock is the best tier 2 pet there is. Its attack power increases as it gets hurt and if paired with a supporting pet can buff the peacock's health, it can absolutely wreck another player's team.

Level 1: Hurt: Gain +4 Attack.
Level 2: Hurt: Gain +8 Attack.
Level 3: Hurt: Gain +12 Attack.

Shop Tier: 6, Stats: 6/4

The tiger is a general crowd favorite and an icon of the game itself. It works well practically on any team which has space for it because it allows the pet in front of it repeat their abilities. Essentially, if you put it behind your best pet, your teams becomes twice as good

Level 1: The friend ahead repeats their ability in battle as if they were level 1.
Level 2: The friend ahead repeats their ability in battle as if they were level 2.
Level 3:The friend ahead repeats their ability in battle as if they were level 3.

Number 5

Tiger

Number 4

Guineafowl

Shop Tier: 3, Stats: 2/4

The Guineafowl is the most controversial pet I have on my list and it ranks number 4. This is because with the right team, it can become one of the most broken pets in the game. Its mechanic is to earns trumpets everytime it gets hurt and later have those trumpets used to summon a golden retriever with the attack and health equal to the number of trumpets. If you get a guineafowl that can take a lot of hits, you're new pet dog will do the rest.

Level 1: Hurt: Gain +2 Trumpet.
Level 2: Hurt: Gain +4 Trumpet.
Level 3: Hurt: Gain +6 Trumpet.

Shop Tier: 6, Stats: 6/6

The Snake is my third faveorite pet in the game. It attacks a random pet additionally whenever the pet infront of it attacks. It makes a great addition to many team comps and often seems to be a main winning factor in late game battles.

Level 1: Friend ahead attacks: Deal 5 Damage to one random enemy.
Level 2: Friend ahead attacks: Deal 10 Damage to one random enemy.
Level 3: Friend ahead attacks: Deal 15 Damage to one random enemy.

Number 3

Snake

Number 2

Monkey

Shop Tier: 5, Stats: 1/2

The secret to winning in Super Auto Pets is to make the pets on your team very strong. Many pets are able to do this in some round about fasion, but none may do this better than the monkey, and that is why it get number 2 on my list.

Level 1: End turn: Give right-most friend +2 Attack and +3 Health.
Level 2: End turn: Give right-most friend +4 Attack and +6 Health.
Level 3:End turn: Give right-most friend +6 Attack and +9 Health.

Shop Tier: 6, Stats: 4/5

No pet helps other pets beef up better than the monkey...except for maybe my number 1 pick, WHICH IS THE CAT!!! So far, food items have not been mentioned in this list; however, food items are often the main way additional health and attack is added onto pets in the game. What the cat does is it scales the amount of health and atttack given from food items. This allows your pets to become insanely tanky and powerful in perhaps the quickest way in Super Auto Pets.

Level 1:Food gives double health and attack. Works 2 times per turn.
Level 2: Food gives triple health and attack. Works 2 times per turn.
Level 3: Food gives quadruple health and attack. Works 2 times per turn.

Number 1

Cat
