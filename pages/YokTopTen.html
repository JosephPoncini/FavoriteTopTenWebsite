The Top 10:

Kyle Yok Eyh Ma's Picks


Number 10

Bison

Shop Tier: 4, Stats: 4/4
Level 1, End turn: If this has a level 3 friend, gain +1 Attack and +2 Health.
Level 2, End turn: If this has a level 3 friend, gain +2 Attack and +4 Health.
Level 3, End turn: If this has a level 3 friend, gain +3 Attack and +6 Health.

The bison is a classic example of a 'win-more' type of pet and can easily auto-pilot you to a 10 piece win if all the conditions are met in a timly matter to get the Bison ahead over any enemy pet. All you need to start the ramping up the unit is a level 3 friend. Leading into round 5 you can save you pet level ups to have a chance to get a early Tier 4. Since you are already leveling up pet to fish for a tier 4 the Bison it goes hand in hand with this win condition.

Number 9

Oyster

Shop Tier: 6, Stats: 2/10
Level 1, Sell: Gain +3 Gold
Level 2, Sell: Gain +6 Gold
Level 3, Sell: Gain +9 Gold

Besides being super cute (as stated by Ken himself) there is almost never a reason not to buy this unit. The level 1 Oyster sells for +3 gold but you also get +1 Gold whenever you are selling a level 1 pet. So, buy-selling will always net +1 Gold. This may seem insignificant but you techinally get a free shop refreseh and if you are at Tier 6 Shop you can keep buying and selling Oysters. This will help you cycle to level up the pets on your team. Only flaw is that it has no value in battle.

Number 8

Siberian Husky

Shop Tier: 5, Stats: 4/3
Level 1 End Turn: Give friends with no held food +1 Attack and +1 Health.
Level 2 End Turn: Give friends with no held food +2 Attack and +2 Health.
Level 3 End Turn: Give friends with no held food +3 Attack and +3 Health.

The Huskey is a super fun unit that is easy to play. You get super scaling at the price of losing food perks but that is not a deal breaker if you can find a win fast enough. Another perk of the Huskey is that you can chain multiple of them. As long as none of your Huskeys are holding foods, they will buff each other. A team of 5 Huskeys is a viable meta team.

Number 7

Monkey

Shop Tier: 5, Stats: 1/2
Level 1 End turn: Give right-most friend +2 Attack and +3 Health.
Level 2 End turn: Give right-most friend +4 Attack and +6 Health.
Level 3 End Turn: Give right-most friend +6 Attack and +9 Health.

Monkey business is necessary to have fun. The monkey is very low commitment for a very high reward so what could be better. This unit can easliy propell a carry unit to 50/50 and after that unit is maxed you simply change your order and pump up another friend. Even in a pinch you can have the Monkey make itseld stronger by placing him in the right-most slot. The Monkey has also gone through many different icons but all of them have look pretty nice.

Number 6

Nyala

Shop Tier: 5, Stats: 3/4
Level 1 Faint: Gain +8 Trumpets
Level 2 Faint: Gain +16 Trumpets
Level 3 Faint: Gain +24 Trumpets

Trumpets are a fairly new mechanic introduced to Super Auto Pets and the Nyala is the best unit to gain them with. Trumpets will summon a Golden Retriever with the same Attack and Health as the amount of trumpets that you have. Using other foods and pets, you can easily trigger the Nyala effect multiple times and gain access to a 50/50 Golder Retriever. The ease of use is what puts this pet so highly on the list and in combination with other Friends it can take a losing team to a winning state.

Number 5

Tiger

Shop Tier:6, Stats: 6/4
Level 1 The friend ahead repeats their ability in battle as if they were level 1.
Level 2 The friend ahead repeats their ability in battle as if they were level 2.
Level 3 The friend ahead repeats their ability in battle as if they were level 3.

Tiger plays with one of the most fun mechanics in the game, repeating abilities. Pairing the Tiger with certain other pets can be unfair but it can also be completely useless if you do not the the right pairs. Being able to have multiple triggers in battle enables snipers to one-shot teams and can also enable insane buffs. There is a reason why the Tiger has been a fan-favorite since the beginning of the game.

Number 4

Cat

Shop Tier:6, Stats: 4/5
Level 1 Food gives double health and attack. Works 2 times per turn.
Level 2 Food gives triple health and attack. Works 2 times per turn.
Level 3 Food gives quadruple health and attack. Works 2 times per turn.

Meoooooowwwwwwwwwww. That is all. But seriously the multiplier that the Cat gives is unfair. Using foods like pizza and sushi that hit multiple pets in a single shop buy further amplifies the multiplier. You dont even need to get the Cat to level 3 to get the x4 effect. Simply buy more Cat's and buy more foods and you team will instantly get stronger. Cute unit, Power effect. Only downside is that it is a tier 6 and if you are on a bad run seeing the Cat too late might not be able to save you.

Number 3

Cow

Shop Tier:5, Stats: 4/6
Level 1 Buy: Replace shop food with 2 free Milk that gives +1 Attack and +2 Health.
Level 2 Buy: Replace shop food with 2 free Milk that gives +2 Attack and +4 Health.
Level 3 Buy: Replace shop food with 2 free Milk that gives +3 Attack and +6 Health.

Moooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooo! The cow is the only unit in the game that is a MUST buy every time you see it. You buy it and instantly get 2 free milks to buff your team. Its a unit and food perk in one. Using other pets like the Cat or Seal it can start some insane combos to sky rocket the stats on your team. There is no scaler that is more efficient and if you are not committed to level up the cow you can basically buy and sell the cow for a +2/+4 buff that only costs 2 gold.

Number 2

Dragon

Shop Tier: 6, Stats: 6/8
Level 1 Tier 1 friend bought: Give friends +1 Attack and +1 Health.
Level 2 Tier 1 friend bought: Give friends +2 Attack and +2 Health.
Level 3 Tier 1 friend bought: Give friends +3 Attack and +3 Health.
